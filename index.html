How we work

We collaborate with the stakeholders, understand their expectations and put it all into a SRS (software requirements document). This normally takes anywhere from a day for a simple project to a month for really complex projects.

Once all the stakeholders have signed off on the SRS we come up with mockups for every unique workflow, for complex projects we take this a step ahead and create prototypes which look identical to the final software but with dummy hard coded data. This step can take anywhere from a day to 2 weeks.

Once we have signed off on the mockups we provide you with a SOW (statement of work) which calls out the scope of work, identifies the people who would be working on the software, the time lines which include the key feature milestone dates , go-Live dates and the Service Level Agreements (SLA). This step can take anywhere from two days to a week.

Once you sign off on the Statement of work (SOW) we assign a single point of contact (SPOC) for the project who would be responsible for the execution of the project.

The SPOC introduces you to the team in a project kick-off meeting

We follow SCRUM or Waterfall which are the industry standards for software development world wide. In accordance the SPOC will schedule weekly meetings which would cover project updates and demonstration of the features completed in the current week via screen sharing. At the end of the weekly meeting you would be emailed the URL for accessing the demoed software remotely and you can pass on the link to your colleagues thus giving you a chance to provide us your feedback early.

We progress with the development of your software and we sign off on every milestone reached until we reach a feature complete.

Our team gives you time to complete your "user acceptance testing" (UAT) cycles to ensure that the software has no major defects, if there are any we fix them as you report them on our online defect tracking tool. This stage can take a week to 20 days.

Once you are satisfied that the software has no defects our team provides you with technical assistance and documentation for deployment and going live.

We continue to support your software after you go live, adding features or making changes which you feel will help your business.
